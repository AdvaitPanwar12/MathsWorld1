Additon

For class 1 -

Addition is basically adding of numbers using your fingers, doing it in a paper etc. Addition can be useful in real life as it is needed for day to day calculations.
Lets take this for a example- 1 Apple + 1 Apple ( Keep in Mind that this sign (+) called plus is used for addition purposes.) So here, we can see that 1 apple is being
added to one more apple. If you take one apple in your hand and your mother gives you one more, then you will have 2 apples. Now you can see that the number
increases as we move forward. Now this was only 1 apple and 1 apple. Now think you have 2 oranges and your father gives you 2 oranges more. You will have
4 apples as 2 more are added. Now lets see how we can do it in paper.

2 + 2 = 4
